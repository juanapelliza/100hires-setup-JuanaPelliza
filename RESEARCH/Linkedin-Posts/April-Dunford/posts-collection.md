# April Dunford: LinkedIn Content Collection

## Post 1: Sales and Marketing Alignment in Positioning
**Link**: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/aprildunford_you-dont-want-to-start-any-positioning-work-activity-7422666982032056322-QbQr

**Topic**: Positioning Strategy & Internal Alignment

**Analysis**: April highlights a critical mistake in B2B SaaS: treating positioning as a "marketing-only" project. She argues that you shouldn't even start positioning work if you don't have the sales team in the room. If marketing creates a story that sales doesn't actually use in their pitches, the positioning is effectively dead. In 2026, positioning must be a unified strategy that bridges the gap between how a product is marketed and how it is sold.

**Key Takeaway**: Positioning is a team sport. Its success isn't measured by how good the website looks, but by whether the sales team feels it helps them win deals. Without sales and marketing alignment, positioning is just a creative exercise with no business impact.
