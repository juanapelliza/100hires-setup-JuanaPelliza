# Amanda Natividad: LinkedIn Content Collection

##Post 1: The Evolution of Search
**Link**: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/amandanat_search-isnt-dead-its-so-alive-that-it-activity-7445492752856883200-tUyN

**Topic**: SEO & Modern Search Behavior

**Analysis**: Amanda debunks the idea that SEO has died with the rise of AI. She argues that search is “more alive than ever,” but it has become fragmented: people now search on LinkedIn, Reddit, and social platforms. Content needs to be where intent happens, not just on Google.

**Key Takeaway**: For B2B marketing, being “discoverable” in 2026 means creating native content on social platforms that feeds AI answer engines and social search.

##Post 2: Zero-Click Marketing Framework
**Link**: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/amandanat_if-you-want-to-try-zero-click-marketing-for-activity-7442261462473150464-QfTK

**Topic**: Content Distribution Tactics

**Analysis**: In this post, Amanda provides a practical guide on how to execute “Zero-Click Marketing.” She explains that delivering the main value directly in the LinkedIn post—without forcing the user to click on an external link—generates much more trust and algorithmic reach.

**Key Takeaway**:Don’t save the “secret” for the end of a post or for an external blog. Deliver the solution in the first lines of the post to optimize content consumption in the feed.
