# Gia Laudi: LinkedIn Content Collection

## Post 1: AI Features vs. Resonant Messaging
- **Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/georgianalaudi_ai-features-but-your-messaging-feels-off-activity-7338580633058697216-yI6f
- **Topic:** SaaS Messaging & AI Strategy
- **Analysis:** Gia points out a critical mistake in 2026: SaaS companies are rushing to add "AI features" but forgetting to update their messaging. If your customers don't understand how the AI helps them achieve their *specific goal* (Job-to-be-Done), the feature is just noise. 
- **Key Takeaway:** Messaging shouldn't lead with "We have AI." It should lead with the problem being solved, and mention AI only as the "how." Customer-led growth means focusing on the *value*, not the *tech stack*.
