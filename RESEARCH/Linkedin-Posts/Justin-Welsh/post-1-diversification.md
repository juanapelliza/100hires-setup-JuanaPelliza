# LinkedIn Post: The Danger of Single-Platform Dependency
**Author:** Justin Welsh
**Topic:** Audience Diversification

## Post Content
"If your entire business lives on a platform you don’t own, you don’t have a business. You have a job working for an algorithm.

I spent 2 years mastering LinkedIn. It was my only source of income. Then I realized:
- Reach can drop overnight.
- Accounts can get restricted.
- Policies change.

Now, my LinkedIn strategy has one goal: Awareness.
But my business strategy has another: Ownership.

I use LinkedIn to drive people to my newsletter. 
On LinkedIn, I am a guest. In my newsletter, I am the host.

Diversify or die."

## Analysis
This post directly correlates with the "Creator Funnel" mentioned in the research interview, specifically the transition from Awareness to Trust.
