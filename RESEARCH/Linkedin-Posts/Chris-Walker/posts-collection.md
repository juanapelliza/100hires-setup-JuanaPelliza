## Post 1: AI vs. Skilled Workers
- **Link:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/chriswalker171_imagine-trying-to-tell-gary-the-factory-worker-activity-7446230422101045248-2-z7?utm_source=share&utm_medium=member_desktop&rcm=ACoAAEIs1ZoBiILZxqd8jn9_hxYmfh1DV4uWbIw
"Now consider that many people reading this are Gary.
They've spent 5-20 years building a career as a knowledge worker. Making good money. Respected in their field.
Their company started implementing AI in 2023.
They can see their team shrinking. Friends at other companies are getting laid off.
So they're working harder than ever. Starting early. Available late. Learning every new AI tool they can find.
But knowledge work is being automated. The premium is moving up a level again to the people who have developed what AI cannot replicate. Clarity. Self-trust. Emotional Sovereignty. Equanimity. Creativity. Vision. Presence.
The shift isn't about working harder or learning more tools to do knowledge work better.
It's about elevating to the next level. Again."
