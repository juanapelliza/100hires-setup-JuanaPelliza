# Chris Walker: 2026 LinkedIn Insights

## Post 1: AI vs. Strategy
"AI can write your post, but it can't have your experience. 
The reason your LinkedIn engagement is dropping is because you're using AI to sound like everyone else. 

In 2026, the 'Average' is a death sentence. 
Be polarizing. Have an opinion. Share a failure. 
That is what drives demos, not optimized keywords."

## Post 2: The New B2B Funnel
"The linear funnel is officially dead. 
Your customers are learning about you through LinkedIn videos and Slack communities 6 months before they ever visit your website. 

If you aren't creating 'Narrative-Driven' content today, you aren't just late—you're invisible."
