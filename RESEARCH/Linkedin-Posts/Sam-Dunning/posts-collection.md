# Sam Dunning: LinkedIn Content Collection

##Post 1: When Not to Do SEO/AEO
**Link**: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/samdunning_when-not-to-do-seoaeo-3-real-scenarios-activity-7449539482682343424-Dzae

**Topic**: SEO Strategy & Timing

**Analysis**: Sam breaks down the realistic side of Search Engine Optimization and Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) in 2026. He argues that SEO is not a "magic pill" for every business. If a company has a low budget, needs immediate cash flow, or is in a brand-new category where nobody is searching for their solution yet, investing heavily in SEO/AEO is a strategic mistake.

**Key Takeaway**: SEO is a long-term play for compounding growth. If your business survival depends on sales this month, you should prioritize outbound or paid social over search-driven content.

##Post 2: The Trio Driving Bookings
**Link**: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/samdunning_forget-the-funnel-this-trio-is-driving-booking-activity-7446659576545865728-s1Z6

**Topic**: Demand Generation & Conversions

**Analysis**: Sam challenges the traditional "funnel" concept by introducing a simplified trio of tactics that actually result in demo bookings. He emphasizes that in the current B2B landscape, buyers don't follow a linear path. Instead, success comes from a combination of high-intent social content, frictionless CTAs, and consistent presence in the platforms where your audience already hangs out.

**Key Takeaway**: Stop trying to "trap" leads in a complex funnel. Focus on being the most helpful and visible resource in your niche, making it as easy as possible for them to book a call when they are ready.
