# Sam Dunning: LinkedIn Content Collection

## Post 1: The "Pitch Slap" 
"Stop 'Pitch Slapping' people the moment they accept your connection request. 

Acceptance ≠ Permission to sell.

If you walked into a bar and a stranger asked you to marry them, you'd run. LinkedIn is the same. Start with a question about their business. Be a human first, a salesperson second. 

The best 'pitch' is a great profile and a helpful comment."

## Post 2: Why your SaaS isn't growing
"Your SaaS isn't growing because your content is about YOU.
- Our new feature.
- Our new office.
- Our team lunch.

Your customers have problems. Talk about the problems. 
Show them the cost of NOT solving those problems. 
Content that educates is content that sells."
