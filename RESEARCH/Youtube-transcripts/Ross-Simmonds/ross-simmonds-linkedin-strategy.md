## Summary
- **The "Native Content" Advantage**: Ross emphasizes that the LinkedIn algorithm rewards content that keeps users on the platform. Direct links to external blogs are penalized.
- **Hook-Value-CTA**: This is the "B2B Holy Trinity." Start with a scroll-stopping hook, provide actionable value in the middle, and end with a clear Call to Action.
- **Distribution First**: Don't just post; distribute. Turning one core insight into multiple formats (text, carousels, video) is the only way to stay top-of-mind.

## Key Insights
1. **Stop the Scroll**: Your first sentence is your "headline." If it doesn't hook the Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) in 2 seconds, the post fails.
2. **Carousel Authority**: Carousels are the best way to explain complex SaaS features because they increase "dwell time" (the time a user spends on your post).
3. **Double Down on "Saves"**: Likes are vanity metrics. "Saves" and "Shares" are the real signals that your content is solving a problem for your audience.
