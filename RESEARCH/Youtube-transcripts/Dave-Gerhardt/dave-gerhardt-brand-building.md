## Summary
- **The Brand Moat:** Dave explains that features can be copied in 24 hours, but a brand cannot. Brand is the only sustainable competitive advantage in B2B SaaS.
- **Copywriting is Marketing:** Most B2B marketing fails because the writing is stiff. Dave advocates for "writing like you talk" to build an immediate connection with the reader.
- **The Power of Narrative:** It’s not about what your product does; it’s about the "New World" your customer lives in and how you help them win in it.

## Key Insights
- **Show Your Face:** Brands are no longer faceless entities. The most successful SaaS companies in 2026 have founders or employees who are active and "human" on LinkedIn.
- **Niche Down to Blow Up:** Don't try to be everything to everyone. Pick a very specific group of people, solve their specific problem, and own that conversation.
- **Feedback Loops:** Use social media as a real-time testing ground for ideas. If a LinkedIn post gets a lot of engagement, turn that idea into a blog post or a webinar.
