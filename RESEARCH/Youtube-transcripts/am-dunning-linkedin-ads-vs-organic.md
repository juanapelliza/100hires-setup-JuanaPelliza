## Summary
- **The "Help over Sell" Rule**: Sam argues that SaaS companies fail on LinkedIn because they only post "Book a Demo" links instead of solving problems in the feed.
- **Consistency is a Signal**: Posting 3-5 times a week isn't just for reach; it's to stay top-of-mind when the buyer is actually ready to buy.
- **Profile as a Sales Deck**: Your profile shouldn't be a CV; it should be a landing page that answers: "What problem do you solve?"

## Key Insights
1. **Zero-Click Content**: Give the value away in the post. Don't force people to click a link to learn the "secret."
2. **The "Relevant Reach"**: It's better to have 100 views from SaaS Founders than 10,000 views from random people.
3. **Engaging is Part of the Job**: You don't just post; you must spend 15 mins a day commenting on your target customers' posts.
