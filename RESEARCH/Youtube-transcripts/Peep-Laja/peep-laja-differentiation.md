## Summary
- **The "Better" Trap**: Most SaaS try to be "better" than competitors. Peep argues you must be "different" or "only." 
- **Message Resonance**: If your LinkedIn post could be written by any of your competitors, it’s a waste of money/time.
- **Category Entry Points**: You need to be the first brand that comes to mind when a specific problem arises.

## Key Insights
1. **Differentiate or Die**: In a crowded market, the product that is easiest to understand and remember wins, not necessarily the one with more features.
2. **Copywriting for SaaS**: LinkedIn content should focus on the "New Way" of doing things vs. the "Old Way."
3. **The "Check" Test**: Look at your LinkedIn headline. If your competitor could use the same headline, change it immediately.
