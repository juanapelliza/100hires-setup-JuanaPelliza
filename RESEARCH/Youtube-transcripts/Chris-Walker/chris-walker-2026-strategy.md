## Summary
- **AI-Driven Efficiency**: Chris explains that in 2026, volume is cheap because of AI. To stand out, you need a "Unique Point of View" (POV) that AI cannot replicate.
- **The Shift to Quality**: Buyers are overwhelmed with AI-generated spam. Organic LinkedIn strategy now must focus on high-authority, human-led insights.
- **Agile GTM**: Smaller SaaS companies have an advantage because they can pivot their content strategy faster than $100M+ corporations.

## Key Insights
1. **Human-in-the-loop**: Use AI for research, but the "final hook" and the "strategic opinion" must be human to build trust on LinkedIn.
2. **Community over Reach**: It's no longer about how many people see your post, but *who* engages with it. 
3. **Owning the Narrative**: If you don't define the problem your SaaS solves, your competitors (or AI) will do it for you.
