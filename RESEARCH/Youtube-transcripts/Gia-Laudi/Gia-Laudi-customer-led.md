## Key Insights
1. **Listen Before You Write**: The best LinkedIn posts are just paraphrased versions of what customers say in sales calls or support tickets.
2. **The "Struggle" Hook**: Most SaaS companies start posts with features. Gia suggests starting with the *struggle* the customer is feeling right now to build instant empathy.
3. **Success Milestones**: Instead of just aiming for a "sign up," your content should help the customer reach their next milestone in their own journey.
