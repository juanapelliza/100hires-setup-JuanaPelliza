## Summary
- **Distribution is not an Afterthought**: Amanda explains that you should think about how a post will live on LinkedIn *while* you are writing the core content.
- **The Zero-Click Philosophy**: Algorithms want to keep users on-site. If you help the algorithm, the algorithm helps you with reach.
- **Content Efficiency**: How to take one high-effort piece and turn it into multiple "native" insights for the LinkedIn feed.

## Key Insights
1. **The "Punchline First" Framework**: Don't build up to a conclusion at the end of a long post. Give the value in the first two lines to stop the scroll.
2. **Platform Native Experience**: Instead of asking people to leave LinkedIn to read a thread or a blog, bring the "meat" of that content directly into the post.
3. **Engagement as Distribution**: Replying to every comment in the first hour isn't just polite; it's a distribution tactic that tells LinkedIn the post is "hot."
